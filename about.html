About us

Get to know | Campsite

Vision

We aim to build a vibrant community. We envision a space where knowledge and experience can be freely shared, promoting continuous learning and growth. We stand together for a truly collaborative experience making a positive contribution to the world around us.

Values

Coming together

Our goal with Campsite is to create a community among young campervan users. In our interviews, we learned that while our audience would like to make more friends while travelling, they are unable to do so because they are either afraid to approach one another or do not know where to look. For this reason, we want to bring travellers together so they can become friends while travelling. Through blogs on our website, travellers can share their experiences, and other users can respond by asking questions about hotspots and other things. On top of that if they're in the same location, they can also get in touch with them and meet up with them, so that they can create unforgettable memories. And even become long-term travel friends. That is how Campsite brings people together.

Sharing knowledge and experiences

We discovered during the interviews that many respondents felt uninformed about the place they were visiting. because there is not one place where you can find all the information about the locations. All the information is spread throughout the internet. Herein lies the role of Campsite. Users of our platform can use blogs and videos to share their experiences, hotspots they've visited, and other camper knowledge. When others travel to that location, they can respond by asking about the experience or by taking their experiences into account when they are visiting that place. So that they can get the most out of their travel.

Safe environment

We want our users to feel safe while using our platform. We want to do this by inspiring empathy and respect in others. Make users feel free to express themselves authentically without fear of judgement, by fostering inclusivity and awareness. We cultivate an environment in which every person's voices and dignity count through community legislation. Safety also involves monitoring. We recognize that for users to fully engage and share their experiences, they must feel safe and supported. To that end, every post that seems out of the ordinary or raises questions will be manually reviewed by our team.

Brand Name

Our company name, Campsite, This fits with our target audience because it is a portmanteau of the terms "website" and "camper." However, it also includes the word campsite, referring to a location where camping is permitted. Our target audience loves camping and therefore it fits with our audience. In order to make our logo memorable, we made it very simple.
