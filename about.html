About us

Get to know | Campsite

Vision

We aim to build a vibrant community. We envision a space where knowledge and experience can be freely shared, promoting continuous learning and growth. We stand together for a truly collaborative experience making a positive contribution to the world around us.

Values

Coming together

At Campsite, we're all about connecting young campervan enthusiasts like you! We understand that it can be difficult or akward to make friends while travelling. Maybe you just don't know where to look, or maybe you're uncomfortable approaching other travellers. But don't worry, Campsite is here to help with that! Our goal is to bring together travellers just like you so that you can make friends and maybe even discover the world together. How? It's all about exchanging experiences, after all! You can read blogs written by other travellers on our website, where they share travel experiences and advice on where to go. The best part is that you can now participate in the conversation! Make connections with people who share your enthusiasm for adventure, exchange ideas, and pose questions. But there's still more! It's simple to get in touch with other Campsite users and set up a meetup if you happen to be in the same area. Imagine the opportunities: bonding over similar experiences, travelling to new locations together, and making lifelong memories. Who knows? Along the way, you might even make a lifelong travel companion. Come join us at Campsite if you're prepared to go on amazing adventures and form relationships that go beyond the road.

Sharing knowledge and experiences

Wasting endless hours in front of a computer to learn even the tiniest bit about hotspots for your journey. Isn't that annoying? It can be challenging to find trustworthy insights when information is spread throughout the whole internet. But do not worry, as Campsite is here to save the day! Consider Campsite to be your one-stop shop for anything travel-related. Users can explore blogs and videos here that are brimming with first-hand knowledge, insider advice, and must-see locations. You can find everything you're looking for in one handy location, including the best hiking trails, undiscovered attractions, and nearby restaurants. But that's not all: a Campsite aims to build a community of travellers who share similar interests in addition to imparting knowledge. You can benefit from the combined experience of other travellers when you visit a place that we have featured on our platform. Ask questions, look for advice, or just take in the experience and knowledge of those who have been there before. Campsite takes care of discovering your road's mysteries and make sure every journey for you is nothining but spectacular.

Safe environment

Your security and comfort are our main concerns at Campsite. We want our platform to be a friendly environment where each user is valued, respected, and free to be who they are. It's encouraged for you to be authentic here, without worrying about criticism. We think it's important to embrace diversity and each person's distinct viewpoint. We make sure that every voice is heard and that everyone's dignity is maintained by enforcing community rules and policies. However, safety is about more than just that. It is also about cyber security. For this reason, we're dedicated to continuously monitoring our platform to maintain a welcoming and safe atmosphere for everyone. We are able to do this because we possess an SSL licence, which shields you from all the online hackers. Our committed team will also intervene to look into and take appropriate action if something looks strange or causes you to have concerns. On Campsite we want to make sure you not only feel safe but that you really are safe.

Brand Name

Our company name, Campsite, This fits with our target audience because it is a portmanteau of the terms "website" and "camper." However, it also includes the word campsite, referring to a location where camping is permitted. Our target audience loves camping and therefore it fits with our audience. In order to make our logo memorable, we made it very simple.
